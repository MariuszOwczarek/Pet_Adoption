Our Purpose & Principles
Guided by Compassion

Championing the cause of the most vulnerable animals while enriching the lives of pets and people. This fundamental belief serves as our guiding light, directing every endeavor to maximize our positive influence.

Principled No-Kill Philosophy™

Upholding our promise never to euthanize a pet due to space or duration. Programing Guys Society (PGS) stands as a haven for the most fragile creatures—those who are sick, injured, or abused, often rejected elsewhere. By embracing these pets, we've substantially reduced euthanasia by 83%. Nevertheless, in exceptional cases where an animal suffers from severe ailments or poses a threat to others, humane euthanasia becomes an act of compassion.

Enlist in Our Cause

Daily, the Programing Guys Society confronts unimaginable scenarios—pets emerging from realms of abuse, neglect, and abandonment. Although we can't rescue them all, not a day passes without our efforts to do so.

Yet, none of this lifesaving work is possible without the generosity of our donors. As a local nonprofit unsustained by governmental bodies or other humane societies, our vital work relies on our community's benevolence. Support us today and secure a future for pets in need.
